Privacy

KPIFit Training is designed to work without sending your full training history to a KPIFit Training-owned database. The goal is: you get analytics, without giving up control of your data.

What KPIFit Training processes

  • Workout logs you import (CSV) or sync via supported integrations.
  • Derived analytics such as training volume, PRs, and trend summaries.

Where your data is processed

Analytics are computed locally in your browser. This includes charts, summaries, and per-exercise history.

What KPIFit Training stores

  • Data you choose to keep in your browser (for example via local storage) so the app can load faster next time.
  • Preferences (such as weight units and body map settings).

What KPIFit Training does not store on a KPIFit Training server

  • Your complete workout history as an account profile.
  • Your derived analytics as a server-side dataset.

Important limitation

If you use a third-party data source (for example an API sync), that third-party service may have its own privacy and security model. KPIFit Training’s privacy guarantee is about what KPIFit Training itself stores.
